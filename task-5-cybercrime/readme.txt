Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness

Cybercrime refers to illegal activities carried out using computers, internet, or digital devices.

Types of Cybercrime:
1. Phishing – Fake emails or messages to steal personal data
2. Hacking – Unauthorized access to systems
3. Identity Theft – Using someone else's personal information
4. Cyberbullying – Harassing someone online
5. Online Fraud – Scams through websites or apps

Prevention Methods:
- Use strong passwords
- Do not share OTP or personal details
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Use antivirus software

Conclusion:
Cybercrime is increasing in today’s digital world. Awareness and safety practices are important to protect ourselves online.
