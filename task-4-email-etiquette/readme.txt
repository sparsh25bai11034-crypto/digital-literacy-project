Task 4: Email Etiquette

Email 1: Request for Assignment Extension

Subject: Request for Extension of Assignment Submission

Dear Sir/Ma’am,

I hope you are doing well. I am writing to request an extension for the assignment submission due to some personal reasons. I kindly request you to grant me a few extra days to complete the work.

Thank you for your understanding.

Yours sincerely,  
Sparsh Gupta


Email 2: Internship Application

Subject: Application for Internship Opportunity

Dear Sir/Ma’am,

I am writing to express my interest in an internship opportunity at your organization. I am a B.Tech student at VIT Bhopal University and I am eager to gain practical experience.

I would be grateful if you could consider my application.

Thank you.

Yours sincerely,  
Sparsh Gupta


Social Media Do’s and Don’ts:

Do’s:
1. Use strong passwords  
2. Keep personal information private  
3. Be respectful online  
4. Share useful content  
5. Verify information before posting  

Don’ts:
1. Do not share sensitive data  
2. Do not use offensive language  
3. Do not click unknown links  
4. Do not spread fake news  
5. Do not misuse social media
